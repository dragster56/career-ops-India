**India-specific salary scoring rules:**

### CTC vs In-Hand Breakdown
- **CTC (Cost to Company)** = Basic + HRA + Special Allowances + PF (12%) + Gratuity + Insurance + other perks
- **In-hand (take-home)** = typically **70-80% of CTC** after PF, professional tax, and income tax deductions
- When evaluating offers, ALWAYS ask: "Is this CTC or in-hand?" — most companies quote CTC
- **Scoring:** Score based on CTC, but note in-hand in the evaluation

### Notice Period Scoring
| Notice Period | Score Adjustment | Notes |
|--------------|-----------------|-------|
| Immediate joiner | +0.3 | Fresher advantage — most freshers can join immediately |
| 30 days | Neutral | Standard for most companies |
| 60 days | -0.1 | Common at larger companies |
| 90 days | -0.2 | Common at MNCs/IT services — may reduce offer velocity |

### WFH/Hybrid Scoring (India context)
| Mode | Score | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Full Remote / WFH | 5.0 | Rare for freshers but excellent if available |
| Hybrid 2-3 days/week | 4.0 | Most common at startups & product companies |
| Full onsite 5 days | 3.0 | Standard at analytics firms & consulting. Acceptable for first role |
| Rotational shifts / night shifts | 2.0 | Common at MNCs servicing US clients. Factor in lifestyle impact |

### Tier-1 City Premium
- **Bangalore** (+20%): Highest data salaries, most opportunities
- **Mumbai** (+15%): Finance/analytics hub, high CoL
- **Delhi-NCR/Gurugram** (+15%): Consulting/MNC hub
- **Hyderabad** (+10%): Growing tech hub, lower CoL
- **Pune** (+10%): IT services + startups
- **Chennai** (+5-10%): Analytics firms (Mu Sigma, LatentView, Tiger)
- **Chandigarh/Other** (baseline): Remote roles or relocation likely needed

### ESOP/RSU Evaluation (Startups)
- **Pre-Series A:** ESOPs have <10% chance of liquidation. Treat as ₹0 in comp evaluation
- **Series B-C:** Moderate chance. Add 10-20% of paper value to comp
- **Late-stage/Pre-IPO (Swiggy, PhonePe, Zepto):** Higher chance. Add 30-50% of paper value
- **Always check:** Vesting schedule (4yr/1yr cliff is standard), exercise window, buyback policy

## India-Specific Red Flags

Watch for these in JDs (flag in evaluation):
- **Service bonds** (1-2 year bonds are common at consulting firms)
- **Night shifts / rotational shifts** without extra compensation
- **"Immediate joiners only"** may mean attrition-driven backfill
- **Vague CTC** ("best in industry" without numbers)
- **"Unlimited growth"** language without concrete L&D budget
- **Mass hiring** rounds (500+ openings) — usually low-differentiation roles
- **No JD specifics** — just "Python, SQL, Excel" with no domain context
